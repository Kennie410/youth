About Youth With Vision Malawi

Youth With Vision Malawi is a youth-led initiative committed to empowering young people with the skills, confidence, and opportunities they need to build a just, inclusive, and prosperous society.

Our Vision

A future where every young person in Malawi is empowered, skilled, and inspired to lead positive change in their community and beyond.

Our Aim

To unlock the potential of young people by promoting creativity, technical skills, leadership development, and human rights awarenessâ€”ensuring no youth is left behind.

What We Do

  • Empower youth through leadership and life-skills training
  • Promote creative arts, culture, and talent development
  • Advocate for human rights, inclusion, and gender equality
  • Support pathways to decent livelihoods and employment
  • Partner with media, community groups, and institutions

Our Plans

We are committed to expanding our reach and impact by:

  • Scaling youth skills and entrepreneurship programs
  • Creating safe spaces for young women and marginalized youth
  • Strengthening partnerships with local and international organizations
  • Using digital platforms and media to amplify youth voices
  • Building sustainable programs that deliver long-term impact
